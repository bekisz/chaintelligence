# Expected In-range Duration

In-range duration is a metric that quantifies the expected duration of a price remaining within a specified range. It is a crucial factor in assessing the risk and potential returns of a trading strategy, particularly in the context of range-bound markets.

## Definition

The in-range duration is calculated fro any any historical day in the time period and calculate the number of days the price was in range.

The expected in-range duration is the average of the in-range duration for all historical days in the time period.

Choose any historical dayly closing price and calculate the in-range duration for for the following days.

Repeat this process for all historical days in the time period.
